Welcome!

The Horseshoe Amateur Radio Club (HARC) is an ARRL-affiliated club located in Blair County Pennsylvania. Our members are active in promoting fellowship and fun among our area amateur radio operators. HARC participates in field day, and has many other special activities of interest to hams. The club is also very active in serving the surrounding communities through providing emergency and public service communications, and also provides classes and testing for those interested in becoming ham radio operators. At an elevation 2552 feet, our club shack on top of Wopsy Mountain provides members with the opportunity to operate on one of the highest points in central Pennsylvania. Either operate one of our club radios or bring your own and connect to one of our antennas to extend your reach further and nail those long-sought-after QSOs. HARC invites all amateur radio operators to join with us. Contact any of our officers for further information.
