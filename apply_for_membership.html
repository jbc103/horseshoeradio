Join HARC

HARC Participates in field day, picnics, hamfests and has many other activities

HARC Provides emergency and public service communications

HARC Promotes Amateur Radio by providing classes and testing for those interested in becoming ham radio operators.

HARC invites all amateur radio operators to join with us. Contact any of our officers for further information.

When you're ready to join, click the image to download the pdf document. Fill it out and return it to the treasurer at the address on the form or at any club meeting.

Club dues are $35 yearly for a regular membership. A spouse can be added for $10 and children (under 18) for $5. The student rate of $15 annually. Dues payments should be paid by January 1st.
