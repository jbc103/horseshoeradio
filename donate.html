Would you like help out the Horseshoe Amateur Radio Club with a donation?

Maybe a donation to the W3SO Project.

Possibly the repeater fund.

Or maybe you would just like to help us out with an open ended tax deductible donation.

If so please follow the link below. You will be taken to PayPal, where you can make a donation using a credit card. You do not need a PayPal account to make a donation.

If you make a donation and would like a receipt for tax purposes, please send an email to Officers@horseshoeradio.com and request a receipt for the sum of your donation, we will glad to provide you with one. You will also receive a receipt from PayPal by email when the transaction is complete.
